Chase The Bull

Instructions

  • You have 60 seconds until the Bull market ends
  • Collect as many 'Bluechip' Coins( / ) to increase your Balance
  • Avoid the animal-based 'Scam-coins' that will make your Balance bleed
  • Use the Arrow Up and Arrow Down Keys to move the player
  • Try your luck when picking up the random Gifts that can add or remove Balance
  • Avoid the Dangerous charging Bear that will instantly make you lose all your Balance

Win Condition:

  • Reach $10,000 Balance within the time limit

Lose Condition:

  • Reach $0 Balance
  • Have less than $10,000 Balance at the end of the bull market
  • Get charged by the Bear
